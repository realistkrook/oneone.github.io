CYBER SECURITY

What are the dangers of being online?

There are many dangers but we'll go over some major ones:

  • IP Trackers: We'll discuss how your IP address can reveal your location and how malicious websites can try to steal it (IP grabbers - explained later).
  • Location Sharing: We'll explore the risks of granting location permissions to websites and apps.
  • Permissions Creep: We'll discuss how seemingly harmless permissions can be misused by websites.
  • Phishing Scams: We'll learn how scammers try to trick you into revealing personal information or downloading malware.
  • Malware We'll explore different types of malware (malicious software) and how they can harm your device and data.
